Waah confidently stated the answer that they had come up with. The stone tablet’s words changed to, “correct!” And disappeared. Waah cheered.
Waah continued on their way, striding down the stone path as quickly as they could. 
Waah kept a watch out for any more stone tablets, but couldn’t see any more.
Waah soon realized that they did not even need to use the map. There was only one clear way for them to go - the stone path.
Eventually, Waah began to feel sleepy. “I should take a break,” they thought. 
So, Waah found a little place that was concealed behind a bush. They took the cloth out of their backpack, spread it out on the dirt, and fell asleep.
