Waah stated the answer that they thought was correct, though their voice was slightly muffled by the rock.
“Incorrect!” The stone tablet read, “remember! If you get three wrong answers-”
“Can you please get this rock off me already?” Waah grumbled.
“Okay.” The stone tablet replied, and it rolled the rock off of Waah.
Waah continued walking until they felt tired. The manure bottle only had a few sips left in it, so Waah knew that they were almost close to the end. 
Waah spread their blanket out on the edge of the path and fell asleep once again.
