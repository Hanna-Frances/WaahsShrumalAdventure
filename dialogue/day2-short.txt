TUESDAY
Waah woke up and continued on their journey. They continued to walk on the stone path, since there really weren't many other places to go.
After a few sips of manure for brunch, they noticed a very tall, large gate blocking their way. It was locked!
The stone tablet appeared again. This time, it said that if they answer the math question right, it will open the gate for them! 
Waah decided to try solving the question.
