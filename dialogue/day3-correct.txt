After several seconds of thinking, Waah stated their answer.
“Correct!” The stone tablet read. It zoomed up into the tree and brought down Waah’s bottle of manure. Waah grinned.
The stone tablet disappeared again. Waah wondered what it was as they continued down the stone path.
They climbed over logs and rocks that were placed on the path, and it was even worse now that Waah was aching all over!
Like the previous two nights - once Waah felt that they could not go on any longer, they found a soft patch of ground on the edge of the path, spread out their blanket and fell asleep.
